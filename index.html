How to Order

Pay with Venmo

Laura takes orders via Venmo @my3dprintshop. Pay here with Venmo .
Include your product name, email address, and shipping address in the Venmo comment section. If you don't have Venmo, just email Laura at laura3dprintshop@gmail.com and pay with cash upon delivery.

FAQ

1) What should I do if I can't find what I want? Please e-mail Laura with your request and she will get in touch.

2) How long does it take to deliver? It can take 1-2 days to print and deliver the item.

3) Can I request another colour? Our selection of colours is limited however, we will do our best to accommodate it but can't promise it. The colours we have are: white, yellow, green, light blue, dark blue, lilac, silky purple, red, brown, pink, black, orange and beige. Let us know in the comments in Venmo if you would like your item in one of these colours

4) Are the items exactly the same colour as in the pictures? Colours of the 3D printed items might be slightly different than in the pictures.

5) Do you print items in more than one colour? Our items are sigle coloured.
